Welcome to UetLibrary

Welcome to the virtual library of the European University of Tirana. In this library you can find the books and scientific research of our pedagogical staff and not only.

Of course, the aim of the university is to be an important institution of higher education focused on teaching and an elite research institution, whose research product is required from the market and continuously improves the teaching and student experience at UET.
